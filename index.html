About Zan Printing.

ZAN PRINTING is a stationery supply and printing entity founded by IBH INVESTMENT in early 2016 under the wings of the Registrar of Companies and was granted Registration Number Z0000058484.

Moreover, ZAN PRINTING has been legally licensed to conduct stationery and printing business by the Municipal Council under Registration Number A2020-5904-035765.

The company has also been registered by the Ministry of Finance and Public Procurement and Disposal of Public Assets as an official supplier for government institutions and was awarded Registration Number 685/G/2020.

Furthermore, ZAN PRINTING is a legally recognized business registered with the Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA) bearing TIN Number 120-779-346.

The company is also registered with the Zanzibar Revenue Board (ZRB) under Registration Number Z025348191.
